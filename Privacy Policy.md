# Privacy Policy for SnapSort

**Last Updated:** June 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SnapSort is a desktop application designed to help you browse, view, and organize your image files. This Privacy Policy explains how SnapSort handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. Data Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

SnapSort does not collect, store, or transmit any data from your computer. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
- Image content or the photos you view
- File metadata, EXIF data, or GPS coordinates
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Crash reports
- Any data that could identify you

### What We Access

SnapSort only accesses:

- Files in folders you manually select for viewing
- File operations (move/delete) only when you initiate them

## 3. How SnapSort Works

SnapSort is a completely offline desktop application:

- No internet connection is ever required or used
- No data is sent anywhere
- No external servers are involved
- No cloud storage integration
- No third-party services are used

All operations occur locally on your computer.

## 4. Data Storage

All images remain on your local hard drive in folders you select. SnapSort does not:

- Create copies of your images
- Store images in any new location without your explicit action
- Save preferences or settings
- Create temporary files

## 5. File Operations

When you use SnapSort:

- **Viewing**: Images are opened directly from your selected folder
- **Moving**: Images are moved to the destination folder you specify
- **Deleting**: Images are permanently removed from your system only when you confirm deletion

All file operations require your direct action.

## 6. Third-Party Access

SnapSort does not:

- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics services
- Integrate with advertising networks
- Use tracking technologies of any kind

## 7. Data Security

Since SnapSort operates entirely offline:

- Your photos stay where you put them
- No data transmission means no risk of interception
- Your data security depends on your own system security

## 8. Your Rights

You have complete control over SnapSort:

- You select which folders to view
- You decide which images to move or delete
- You can stop using the application at any time
- No data is stored, so there is nothing to delete

## 9. Children's Privacy

SnapSort does not:

- Collect any personal information
- Target children
- Require age verification

The application is safe for users of all ages.
